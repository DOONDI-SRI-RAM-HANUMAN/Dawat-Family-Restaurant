Frequently Asked

Good to know

Do you provide takeaway?

Yes — every dish on our menu is available for takeaway. Order via WhatsApp using the floating button.

Do you provide family dining?

Absolutely. We are a dedicated family restaurant with spacious, comfortable seating.

Can I reserve a table?

Yes, call us to reserve. For walk-ins, just scan, select and sit.

Can I order using WhatsApp?

Yes — choose your table, add items to cart and tap “Send Order on WhatsApp”. We handle the rest.

Can I select my table?

Yes! The Smart Table section lets you choose your seat. Your selection is remembered until you change it.
